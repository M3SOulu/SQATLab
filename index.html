Master theses

Hokkari

The guys at hokkari.fi are looking for a master students willing to do their thesis project on their platform.
The project is about interfacing several ice-hockey related databases.
The Hokkari guys are very likely to hire capable students later into the company, and they already are looking for two technical-oriented guys to be hired for future development and maintenance of the platform.
You can contact Raija Halonen (Raija.Halonen@oulu.fi), or directly the Hokkari guys at Juha.Kinnunen@hokkari.fi

TDD DataViz

Me and Burak are looking for a master student who would like to work and write his/her thesis on the topic of Data Visualization.
The aim of the thesis work should be the development of a prototype application (we can decide which kind of) that presents meaningful visualization of unit-test execution results and low-level events logged by Eclipse.
The initial requirements for you will be:

  • Start ASAP (by the end of September)
  • Produce a report of your work by the end of October
  • Try to submit the result of your work to a software engineering conference
    • The department has a small grant (~2000Euro) available for publishing the thesis results

I have already a small prototype (written using D3.js that could be a good starting point for you).
If you are interested please contact me.
